Scratch



Scratch is used for those who are just starting web development, it helps you to understand conditionals. If you are struggling to use scratch, you can follow these tips:

  • Look at other projects

  • Test different ideas
  • Plan before you build

Robotics


Robotics is a great topic where you are able to see some of the code you make come to life, it is important to:

  • Plan what you want your robot to do
  • Practice small steps before making the full action you want your robot to do.
